Everydays

Imagine your life as a ladder. Each rung is a lesson or experience on which you stand. You see further and understand more as you climb the ladder. Through failure and reflection one ensures consistent, albeit non-linear, growth of this fictitious ladder.


My everyday project was a 256-day sprint of productive failure in which I taught myself how to learn. Life became a positive feedback loop, the more I learned the more I wanted to learn.
