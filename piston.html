Piston Waltz

As we move into a more electronic society, we often loose site of the beauty found in mechanical objects. The objective of this short video is to pay homage to the elegance of the piston.


The piston was modeled, rendered, and animated in Cinema 4D. Color correction was done using the Google Nik Collection, and final editing was done in After Effects.

Skills:

3D, Animation, Color Grading
