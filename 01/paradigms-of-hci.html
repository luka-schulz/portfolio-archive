Paradigms of HCI

As we enter the age of entanglement [1] a recognition of our progression into the digital jungle must be made. This project attempts to layout and clarify man's digital journey as described in the Three Paradigms of HCI.


The objective of this presentation is to clearly illustrate the three paradigms of HCI so as to create a level ground for discussion around future interactions.
